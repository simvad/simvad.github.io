Projects

TabletopTunes

TabletopTunes came to be because I got tired of juggling too many Youtube playlists of ambient mood music while running TTRPGs. The desktop-app instead provides you with a simple tagging and querying system that lets you maintain the same vast catalogue of tracks while dynamically adjusting the queue on the fly, based on tag-based queries.

Darwin Games Platform

The Darwin Games Platform is an online platform with simple invitation structure to make setting up a tournament of the Darwin Game as simple as possible.
